The Timeline

A descent into the ripples of a fracturing reality.

Ep. 01

“Odists” and “Vios”

Rex and Fujie wake in a strange parallel world where humans detect and attack Odists. After surviving Silas’s ambush, they meet Enzo, learn the rules, and begin searching for a way out.

Ep. 02

Hostia

Rex and her group narrowly escape human hunters, but lose Silas in the chaos. Four days later, still shaken, they meet Kaida, who leads them to Chester and reveals more about this brutal world, ending in grief and uneasy hope.

Ep. 03

Excito (wakey wakey)

Kaida and Enzo share a quiet moment before the group reunites with Chester, who learns Silas is dead. The evening turns tense with questions, until an ambush by Margot and Bullet throws the base into chaos.

Ep. 04

Damn(um)

Kaida and Enzo survive an attack, but the group is split and their base is destroyed. While Rex spirals with guilt, Margot is lost and Bullet is left isolated, setting the stage for deeper fractures.

Ep. 05

Solus in timore

Bullet’s tragic past reveals family rejection, betrayal, and survival before she found Margot in the parallel world. Back in the present, she breaks under PTSD while Margot returns to Kaida and Enzo, and the group’s fractures deepen.

Ep. 06

Simul

Rex, Fujie, and Chester hide out while Kaida, Enzo, Margot, and Bullet regroup in the forest. As the groups search for stability, old fears, new rules, and quiet betrayals push everyone closer to breaking.

Ep. 07

Clausum

Enzo and Kaida are trapped after Margot and Bullet steal their gun and lock them in, while Rex is baited into a confrontation and Fujie and Chester scavenge the city for supplies.

Ep. 08

Ren

Fujie’s past and the group’s present both turn darker as trust keeps collapsing. Rex is manipulated into danger, Kaida and Enzo remain trapped, and everyone edges closer to panic, hunger, and violence.

Ep. 09

Name

Fujie and Chester head back to the ruined base to search for the others, only to find the Vios still occupying it. Sneaking in, they recover a small part of what was lost.
